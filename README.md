## Features

- 1-Addition
. the calculator allows you to add two numbers together.
. Example: 2 + 2 = 4

- 2-Subtraction
. you can subtract one number from another.
. Example: 2 - 4 = 2

- 3-Multiplication
. multiply two numbers to find their product.
. Example: 2 x 2 = 4

- 4-Division
. divide one number by another (watch out for division by zero!)
. Example: 15 / 3 = 5
